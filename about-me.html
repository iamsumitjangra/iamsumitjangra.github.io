A technology enthusiast who loves to talk about interesting business ideas and deeply interested in cutting-edge technologies, behavioral economics and product design. Along with diverse experiences of working on multiple technology products, evaluating new features, designing architectures and frameworks, he love to work with new teams and embark on new experiences.

Sumit never wanted to be a Software Engineer, but he was destined to be one.

Sumit wanted to be a “wise guy” and knew no pursuit is more empowering, more addicting than tinkering and seeing ideas come to life.

After a strident schooling, he somehow entered into IIT Delhi and yearned to break free and express his creativity. Once learned the power of technology, he never looked back.

To make money Sumit joined Samsung R&D as a Software Engineer. After that he joined Blackboard Radio and to bring change in education at grass root level through technology. Then he started his own startup, CallE which did not work out as expected. Right now he is looking to start an adventuruous ride with some good people.

You can see he is on the right path to be wise. If not please let him know.

Apart from it he loves to read good books & burn some calories. Also learning to play classical guitar. Know more about his extra curricular activities and adventures in the 'Hobbies' section.
